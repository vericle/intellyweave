# Use Cases

**IntellyWeave serves researchers who need professional-grade intelligence analysis without enterprise pricing**

---

## Target Users

### Investigative Journalists

**What they do:**
- Analyze leaked document collections
- Map corruption networks
- Trace financial flows
- Verify source claims

**Example workflow:**
> "I have 50,000 documents from a corporate leak. I need to identify key players, their relationships, and follow the money."

**IntellyWeave provides:** Entity extraction across documents, network visualization of relationships, timeline reconstruction, financial pattern detection.

---

### Historical Researchers

**What they do:**
- Work with declassified archives
- Conduct genealogical investigations
- Research Holocaust and displacement records
- Analyze Cold War history

**Example workflow:**
> "I'm researching displaced persons after WWII. I need to trace individuals across multiple archives in different languages."

**IntellyWeave provides:** Multilingual entity matching, geospatial tracking of movement patterns, cross-archive correlation, identity resolution, **archive discovery** (Quartermaster maps 30+ curated archives), **hypothesis-driven investigation** (Case Officer tests claims against evidence).

---

### Intelligence Analysts

**What they do:**
- Conduct due diligence and background checks
- Perform threat assessment
- Map networks
- Detect patterns

**Example workflow:**
> "I need to verify the background of a potential business partner. What connections and history should concern me?"

**IntellyWeave provides:** Entity network analysis, sanctions checking integration, corporate registry correlation, reputation risk assessment.

---

### NGO Researchers

**What they do:**
- Human rights investigations
- Conflict monitoring
- Corporate accountability research
- Environmental crime tracking

**Example workflow:**
> "I'm documenting a conflict. I need to identify perpetrators, victims, and patterns of violence from witness testimonies."

**IntellyWeave provides:** Entity extraction from testimonies, incident mapping, pattern detection, timeline reconstruction, source attribution.

---

## Investigation Types

### Historical Research

Documents from multiple decades and archives, requiring:
- **Multilingual entity extraction** (German, Russian, English, Portuguese)
- **Identity resolution** across name variations and aliases
- **Geospatial tracking** of movement patterns
- **Timeline reconstruction** from scattered documents
- **Cross-archive correlation** linking disparate sources


---

### Financial Crime Investigation

Cross-border financial crime networks, requiring:
- **Entity extraction** from financial documents
- **Corporate registry searches** across jurisdictions
- **Network visualization** of organizational relationships
- **Blockchain transaction analysis** integration
- **Social media OSINT** for identity verification

**Typical scope:**
- Multiple jurisdictions (UK, Lithuania, Cyprus, Greece, Italy)
- Cryptocurrency wallet tracking
- Shell company network mapping
- Financial flow analysis

---

### Background Verification

Due diligence investigations, requiring:
- **Identity verification** across international databases
- **Sanctions list checking**
- **Social media analysis**
- **Corporate and asset ownership** tracing
- **Historical record verification**

**Typical scope:**
- Persons of interest verification
- Risk assessment based on documentary evidence
- Quick turnaround on verification requests
