## Responsibilities

Maintainers are responsible for:

- Reviewing and merging pull requests
- Triaging issues and feature requests
- Ensuring code quality and consistency
- Helping contributors with their submissions
- Release management

## Becoming a Maintainer

As IntellyWeave grows, we welcome new maintainers. Candidates should demonstrate:

1. **Consistent contributions** - Regular, quality contributions over time
2. **Technical knowledge** - Understanding of the codebase and architecture
3. **Community involvement** - Helpful participation in issues and discussions
4. **Reliability** - Availability and follow-through on commitments

Interested? Start by contributing regularly and engaging with the community. Maintainer invitations are extended based on demonstrated involvement.
