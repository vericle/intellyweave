# Podcast: Il Caso Ingeborg

## Overview

A documentary investigation podcast (15 episodes) exploring the mysterious fate of Ingeborg Louzek, a young Viennese woman arrested in 1950 for espionage during the Cold War. The investigation uses modern OSINT tools and AI-powered analysis to uncover hidden connections in declassified archives.
