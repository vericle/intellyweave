## Overview

This demo showcases IntellyWeave's **Intelligence Analysis Orchestrator** - a 6-phase AI system that extracts entities, maps relationships, performs geospatial analysis, detects patterns, and synthesizes comprehensive intelligence assessments from historical documents about Nazi rat lines.

---

## Intelligence Analysis Phases

### 1. Entity Extractor

![Entity Extractor](01_intelligence_entity_extractor.png)

The **Entity Extractor** phase identifies and extracts 32 entities from documents, including persons (Alois Hudal), organizations, locations, dates, and cryptonyms. Each entity includes confidence scores, source references, and co-occurrences.

---

### 2. Relationship Mapper

![Relationship Mapper](02_intelligence_relationship_mapper.png)

The **Relationship Mapper** phase analyzes connections between extracted entities, identifying 16 relationship patterns including institutional affiliations (Hudal-Vatican), employment relationships (Hudal-CIC), and geographic routing corridors.

---

### 3. Geospatial Analyst

![Geospatial Analyst](03_intelligence_geospatial_analyst.png)

The **Geospatial Analyst** phase identifies 14 geographic intelligence findings, including operational hubs (Vatican City), transit corridors (South Tyrol), embarkation ports (Genoa), and destination settlements (Buenos Aires, Bariloche).
